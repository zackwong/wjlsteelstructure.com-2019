Sandwich Panel Features:

Sandwich panel is made of two layers of forming metal panels with foaming and curing polymer in the middle.Sandwich panels include EPS sandwich panel, rock wool sandwich panel,PU sandwich panel,fiberglass sandwich panel and so on.

Sandwich panel is mainly used in large industrial warehouse, storage,gym, supermarket, hospital, cold storage, trailer, and buildings etc.

Sandwich panel has the features of beautiful shape, colorful gorgeousness and good effect. It can bear loading, keep warm, prevent fire and be waterproof. It has the characters of secondary decoration, convenient installation ,removable and reusable materials and so on.
