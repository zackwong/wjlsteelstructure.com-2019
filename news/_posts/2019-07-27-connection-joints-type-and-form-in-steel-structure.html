1. Connection between steel structure beam and column

1.1 Connection between H-column strong shaft and H-beam: the flange adopts groove welding, and the web plate will be connected by high-strength bolt.

1.2 H Connection between the H-column strong shaft with the cantilever beam and the H-beam: The connection between the cantilever beam section and the column and the intermediate beam section are all welded, and the web plate is fixed by mounting bolts.

1.3 Connection between the H-column strong shaft with the cantilever beam and the H-beam: The cantilever beam section are wholly welded with column, flange welded with the intermediate beam,and the web plate is connected by high-strength bolt.

1.4 Connection between H-column weak axis and H-beam: the flange adopts groove welding, and the web plate will be connected by high-strength bolt.

1.5 H Connection between the H-column weak axis with the cantilever beam and the H-beam: The connection between the cantilever beam section and the column and the intermediate beam section are all welded, and the web plate is fixed by mounting bolts.

1.6 Connection between the H-column weak axis with the cantilever beam and the H-beam: The cantilever beam section are wholly welded with column, flange welded with the intermediate beam,and the web plate is connected by high-strength bolt.

1.7 Connection between box shaped column and H - beam: flange adopts groove welding, and the web plate will be connected by high - strength bolt.

1.8 Connection between box shaped column with the cantilever beam and H-beam: The connection between the cantilever beam section and the column and the intermediate beam section are all welded, and the web plate is fixed by mounting bolts.

1.9 Connection between box shaped column with the cantilever beam and H-beam: The cantilever beam section are wholly welded with column, flange welded with the intermediate beam,and the web plate is connected by high-strength bolt.

1.10Connection between round shaped column with the cantilever beam and H-beam: The cantilever beam section are wholly welded with column, flange welded with the intermediate beam,and the web plate is connected by high-strength bolt.

1.11Connection between cross-shaped column and H-beam: Flange adopts groove welding, and the web plate will be connected by high - strength bolt.

1.12Connection between cross-shaped column with cantilever beam and H-beam: The connection between the cantilever beam section and the column and the intermediate beam section are all welded, and the web plate is fixed by ordinary bolts.

2. Connection between steel structure beam and column hinged connection

2.1 Connection between H-column strong shaft and H-beam: The connection plate and web-plate is connected by high-strength bolt

2.2 Connection between H-column weak axis and H-beam: The connection plate and web-plate is connected by high-strength bolt

2.3 Connection between box shaped column and H-beam: The connection plate and web-plate is connected by high-strength bolt

2.4 Connection between cross-shaped column and H-beam: The connection plate and web-plate is connected by high-strength bolt

2.5 Connection between round-tube shaped column and H-beam: The connection plate and web-plate is connected by high-strength bolt

3. Connection between steel structure main beam and secondary beam

3.1 The simple connection between the H-shaped secondary beam and the H-shaped main beam, the web of the secondary beam extends into the flange of the main beam, and the web plate is connected by high-strength bolts

3.2 The simple connection between the H-shaped secondary beam and the H-shaped main beam, the stiffening rib of main beam extends to connect with secondary beam,and the web plate is connected by high-strength bolts
