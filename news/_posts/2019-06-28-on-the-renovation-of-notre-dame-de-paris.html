Notre Dame Cathedral in Paris was founded in 1163 and completed in 1345. The total height of Notre Dame Cathedral is over 130 meters. It is the first completely Gothic Church in European history. It has epoch-making significance and is also the longest and most representative historic site in Paris. It has been listed on the World Heritage List by UNESCO. It was badly damaged during the French Revolution and rebuilt in the 19th century. Such an ancient building with a history of more than 850 years collapsed in the fire around 6:50 p.m. local time on April 15, 2019. French billionaire Franois Henry Pino said he would invest 100 million euros (about 760 million yuan) to help rebuild Notre Dame in Paris. For the reconstruction of Notre Dame in Paris, people all over the world have different ideas.

Stephen Murray, an art historian at Columbia University, pointed out that Notre Dame de Paris could not be completely restored: the stone of the 12th century was hand-cut by craftsmen, a process that is hard to find today. The timber used on roofs has a history of thousands of years and is hard to find today. Smith believes that the reconstruction of Notre Dame in Paris also has two ideas. In accordance with the principles of the 1964 Venice Charter and with full respect for the original structure, the missing parts can be filled with minimal intervention.

Or, you can refer to accurate historical records, illustrations and photographs (even the 3D digital archives left behind) and use modern technology to reconstruct Notre Dame de Paris accurately and in accordance with the spirit of architecture.

1. The production cycle is short.

2. Assembled accurately and quickly from the beginning to the completion.

3. The vibration (earthquake) resistance and impact resistance of steel are good.

4. The sealing performance of steel structure is good.

5. The heat resistance is high.

6. The material strength is high, the weight is lighter.
